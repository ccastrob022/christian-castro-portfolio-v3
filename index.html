FAQs

Common questions from clients in Canada looking for websites or custom web apps — including HIS/health software, inventory systems, and event ticketing platforms.

Websites (Small Business)

Examples: franksorganicgarden.com and olgapaternina.com.

How long does a business website take?
Typical timelines:
  • Landing page: 3–7 days
  • 5–8 pages: 2–3 weeks
  • Website + blog + forms: 3–5 weeks
It depends on content readiness (text, photos, services list) and approvals.
Do you include SEO?
Yes — I include SEO fundamentals: clean structure, fast load basics, metadata, friendly URLs, mobile responsiveness, and Google Search Console setup. For competitive keywords, we can add a monthly SEO plan.
Can you handle hosting, domain, and emails?
Yes. I can set up hosting, DNS, SSL, and business email (Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace), or work with your existing provider.

Event Ticketing

Ideal for churches, conferences, workshops, and community events: ticket types, QR check-in, capacity control, and reporting.

What features can you build for tickets?
Common features include:
  • Multiple ticket types (free/paid), promo codes
  • Stripe payments, refunds, email confirmations
  • QR codes + check-in dashboard (mobile friendly)
  • Seat/capacity limits, attendee exports (CSV)
  • Admin panel for events, discounts, and staff roles
Can you integrate Stripe and automate emails?
Yes. Stripe integration + webhook automation (confirmation emails, receipts, status updates). If you use Mailchimp or Brevo, I can connect it as well.
Do you provide on-site support for the event?
Remote support is standard. On-site support can be arranged (based on location/date) for large events.

Inventory Systems

For warehouses, small businesses, or multi-location teams: track stock, purchases, transfers, and audit trails.

What does an inventory MVP usually include?
A solid MVP typically includes:
  • Products, categories, suppliers
  • Stock in/out, adjustments, low-stock alerts
  • Locations (bins/warehouses), transfers
  • Role-based access + activity logs
  • Reports: stock valuation, movement, discrepancies
Can you integrate barcodes and scanners?
Yes — barcode support can be done via web (camera scanning) or with standard USB/Bluetooth scanners that behave like keyboard input. We’ll choose the approach based on your workflow.
What database do you recommend?
For most clients: PostgreSQL or SQL Server. If you already run Oracle, we can integrate with it. The choice depends on your existing systems, reporting needs, and budget.

Health Software (HIS / Salud)

Experience supporting business-critical health platforms: reliability, data integrity, auditability, and performance.

Can you build or extend a HIS (appointments, EMR-like workflows, billing)?
Yes. Typical modules include scheduling, patient registry, clinical notes workflows, billing integrations, reporting, and interoperability endpoints. Scope depends on compliance requirements and the target audience (clinic vs. enterprise).
How do you approach security and privacy?
I follow practical security-by-design: least privilege (RBAC), audit trails, encryption in transit (HTTPS), secure secrets management, backups, and logging. For regulated environments, we align to your compliance obligations.
Can you help with performance issues in SQL Server/Oracle?
Yes — I do query profiling, indexing strategy, execution plan analysis, and database structure improvements. I’ve delivered measurable performance gains (e.g., significant reductions in query execution time) through optimization.
